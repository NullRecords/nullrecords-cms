{% trans "Open Source" %}

At its core, OpenBuild helps to build and support new and existing Open Source software for corporate, nonprofits, government and startups who want to build fast, open and quality.

Our teams of two or more provide dedicated support to clients that either directly contract us to support their data management process, development process improvements, migrating to cloud infrastucture and host their Open Source projects/systems.

In addition to our Open Source projects, OpenBuild has two initiatives that promote the use of open data standards and technologies.

{% trans "Please feel free to join our community, or get started with one of the links below."%}

  • Open Build can help you get your business up and running and use cloud native open source software.

  • Start your own company, using free and open source tools and with affordable open source developers.

  • Our partner matching service, we will help you find a developer or development team to build your application.

{% trans "STEM Teaching and Training" %}

With OpenBuild being an active member in the Open Source community, we are deeply passionate about contributing to the spread of learning and knowledge of Open Source technologies, both software and hardware in our local communities.

Our developers, technical specialists, and analysts volunteer their time to work with local organizations and schools to teach STEM based courses.
