New Album: Oscillator Overthruster

📅 2025-07-15 🎵 My Evil Robot Army 💿 Album Release

My Evil Robot Army is back with Oscillator Overthruster — the latest full-length release on NullRecords. This album pushes deep into experimental electronic territory, fusing nu jazz, ambient textures, and glitched-out beats into an immersive sonic experience.

Oscillator Overthruster represents a new direction for MERA, building on the foundations laid by Space Jazz, Travel Beyond, and Spiraling while venturing into uncharted sonic landscapes. The album features rich analog synthesizer work, complex rhythmic structures, and atmospheric production that rewards deep listening.

The album is available now across all major streaming platforms and as a DRM-free lossless download from the NullRecords store.
